Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

Your privacy matters to us, and honestly, we collect as little as we possibly can. Here's exactly what happens to your information when you use My First 100 Foods.

Your tracking stays on your device

The foods you tick off and any custom foods you add are saved only in your own browser, on your own device. That information is never sent to us or stored on a server. You're always in control — clear your browser data, or use the “memory code” to move your list to another device, whenever you like.

Cookies & analytics

We'd love to know which parts of the site are helpful, so we use Google Analytics to see how it's used in aggregate (visits, popular features — never anything that identifies you personally). This only loads after you tap “Accept” on our cookie banner; if you decline, no analytics cookies are set at all. IP addresses are anonymized, and you can change your mind any time by clearing the site's cookies.

When you message us

If you write to us through the contact form, your email address and message are delivered to our support inbox (via a form service called FormSubmit) so that we can write back. We only use it to reply to you.

When you buy something

Payments are handled by our partner Lemon Squeezy, who act as the official seller (Merchant of Record). When you check out, your payment and billing details are collected and processed by them, under their own privacy policy — we never see or store your card details. Your personalized PDF is created right in your browser.

Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live (including under the GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold, or to object to how it's used. Since we hold very little — really just any messages you send us — the simplest way to exercise those rights is to contact us. And remember, most of your data lives on your own device, where you can delete it yourself at any time.

Little ones

This site is made for parents and caregivers, not for children to use directly. We don't knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Staying in touch

We may update this policy as the project grows, and we'll always refresh the date at the top. If you have any privacy question at all, just reach out through the contact form on the site or the support email listed there — we're happy to help.
